That said, here are the KEY TERMS typically important in vendor agreements to watch for:

**Payment & Pricing:**
- Payment terms (Net 30, Net 60, etc.)
- Late payment penalties
- Currency and any FX adjustment clauses
- Volume discounts or tiered pricing
- Price escalation or adjustment mechanisms

**Term & Termination:**
- Contract duration and renewal terms
- Termination for convenience (notice period required)
- Termination for cause conditions
- Obligations upon termination

**Liability & Insurance:**
- Limitation of liability caps
- Indemnification clauses (who covers losses)
- Insurance requirements and coverage amounts
- Warranties and their disclaimers

**Intellectual Property:**
- Ownership of work products or deliverables
- Use of proprietary information
- Confidentiality and NDA requirements
- Rights to feedback or modifications

**Performance & Service Levels:**
- Service level agreements (SLAs) and uptime guarantees
- Remedies for SLA breaches
- Response times and support availability

**General/Administrative:**
- Dispute resolution and governing law
- Force majeure and unforeseen circumstances
- Amendment procedures
- Assignment rights and restrictions
